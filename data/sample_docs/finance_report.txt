DeepChain Finance Insights:
Novatech Solutions (NTS) reported a 15% increase in revenue for Q3 2024, reaching $4.2 billion. 
The growth was primarily driven by their cloud-native payment infrastructure. 
NTS CEO Rajesh Sharma announced a strategic partnership with GlobalBank to expand into Southeast Asian markets.
The company also faces a regulatory review by the SEC regarding its asset valuation methods used in the acquisition of FinPay.
Basel III capital requirements for the company remain steady at 12.5%, well above the minimum threshold.
