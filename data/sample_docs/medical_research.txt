Healthcare Research & Clinical Data:
The efficacy of the new mRNA vaccine candidate (MRNA-202) for pediatric respiratory viruses was evaluated in a Phase II trial.
Dr. Sarah Jenkins led the study at the Global Health Institute.
Results indicated a 92% immune response rate with minimal adverse effects.
HIPAA regulations require that all electronic Protected Health Information (ePHI) related to this trial be encrypted using AES-256.
Access logs for the patient data repository must be audited bi-weekly to ensure compliance with the Security Rule.
The study also explored the impact of social determinants on vaccine accessibility in urban environments.
